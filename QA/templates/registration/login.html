Question Answer Platform

Academic Question-Answer Platform aims at providing teachers and students a comprehensive and inter- active discussion platform.

It has a provision for teachers and students to post answers to an already existing question, add comments, upvote an answer, search for previously posted questions, upload files and allows verification of answers by the teachers.

The questions posted will be categorised topic-wise.
