About Us

Academic Question-Answer Platform aims at provid- ing teachers and students a comprehensive and inter- active discussion platform. This project is an imple- mentation of community based question answering services. It has a provision for teachers and students to post answers to an already existing question, add comments, upvote an answer, search for previously posted questions, upload files and allows verification of answers by the teachers. The questions posted will be categorised topic-wise
